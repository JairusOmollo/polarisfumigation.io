How does it works

A 3-step-pest-control method that uses heating,spraying
and dehydrating to clean your space safely.
Blowing
Heat

The Heater is a device that blows hot air into tiny spaces where bedbug eggs mostly hatch, in order to kill the eggs.

Spraying a Plant Based Liquid

We use a pesticide that is toxic to bugs and harmless to people. The liquid will flush out bedbugs out of hiding and kill them.

Pour an
Absorbing Powder

A desiccant (baking powder-like) destroys the outer coating on a bedbug, it will make the bug slowly dehydrate and die.
